ABOUT DUET

Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET) was established in 1963 as a regional engineering college and later upgraded to a full-fledged university. DUET is known for its quality education, modern facilities and disciplined campus life. Our campus provides a peaceful environment for learning, research and innovation.

We are dedicated to produce skilled, honest and responsible engineers for building a better future.
